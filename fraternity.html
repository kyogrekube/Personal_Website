FIJI Island Week (April 17th - 21st 2023)

FIJI Island Week is a week long set of philantrhopy events that raise money for a preestablished cause. As the current philantrhopy chair, I wanted to push our chapter to raise more money than any individual previous year. Spring 2023 was the 5th ever Island Week at our school, and our most successful year brought in a total of $1,425.00 which went towards the victims of the Fourth St. and Washington St. fires. This fire displaced 9 families and over 25 people. Since 2022, our organization has supported Joesph's House and Shelter, which provides emergency shelter and support services to a yearly average of 3,000 men, women , and children. By the end of the week, our organization raised a total of $2128.02, which by far was our best year.
